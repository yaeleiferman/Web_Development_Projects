Umberto, The Wise Dolphin

Umberto is known as the wisest of all living dolphins. In fact some say that he is the wisest of any dolphin which has ever lived. This may be because Umberto is himself immortal, and has been soaking up wisdom (and seawater) for centuries.

Some of Umberto's favorite things are:

  • avocados
  • repulsive puns
  • fine dining
